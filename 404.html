Short bio

My name is Landen, I currently go to an IB highschool in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I am 15 years old and work in front-end web development. I've worked as a freelance developer as a way to make money in my highschool years for just over a year and a half. I have also worked with Python development on and off for 5 years.

Personal Profile

Apart from my professional goals and aspirations, I love spending time with my friends and family as well as cooking, camping and baseball. I also enjoy the occasional video game to de-stress and relax. Since I was very young I have always enjoyed computers and thought I would love to pursue a job in the field of computer science. I was introduced to computer programming in 6th grade back when I lived in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. I worked on a few different projects along side the teacher that originally introduced me to programming. However this teacher moved to a different school not long after and I lost motivation and ambition. It wasn't until December of 2018 that I rediscovered my love for computer programming. I taught myself web development in Html, Css and Javascript. I worked on many different websites for myself and slowly saw improvement overtime, and not long after I realised I could make the skill marketable.
