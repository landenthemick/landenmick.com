Navigate the collision of Artificial Intelligence and Aviation


Modern Artificial Intelligence was invented ten years ago, and has since completely changed business. On the other hand, we are still flying airliners designed in the 60's. AI has unleashed a world of wealth, understanding, breakthrough and headaches for those that have embraced what it offers.

Deep Sky is here to walk you through the collision of an exponentially powerful technology that is changing at breakneck speed and the needs of ensuring that technology is tamed and directed to work flawlessly in a safety critical industry.
